Unity — Camera Angles & Navigation

Unity · C# · MATLAB · Final Year Project

A study into the relationship between navigational ability and choice of camera angles within game design, conducted as part of my final year university project. A short navigational game was created in Unity containing three levels, each utilising a different popular camera angle, before being subjected to a professional-quality usability evaluation under strict lab settings.

Camera Views

3rd Person, Top Down, and 1st Person camera perspectives used in the study.

Level Design

Data Visualisation

The game featured a script which recorded the player's position and camera direction throughout their playthrough at set intervals. This data was then used to visualise their path and progression using a custom MATLAB function, which could be further analysed alongside the results from the lab evaluation.
